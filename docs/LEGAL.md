These four statements must be expressible from every legal surface in the product.
They are the foundation of LendPaper's liability protection.

1. All generated documents are preliminary estimates only — not offers, commitments,
   or financial advice.
2. LendPaper does not verify, audit, or validate user inputs. Input accuracy is
   solely the responsibility of the originating user (lender/broker/ISO).
3. LendPaper is a software provider only — not a lender, broker, financial
   institution, investment advisor, or regulated financial entity.
4. Final loan terms, rates, fees, and approval decisions are determined solely
   by the funding lender upon completion of underwriting and final review.

## 5. Words and Phrases to Avoid

These terms carry regulatory or legal weight in lending contexts and must not
appear in any generated document, UI copy, or marketing copy without explicit
founder review:

| Avoid | Use Instead |
|---|---|
| "Quote" (for a generated doc) | "Estimate" or "Scenario" |
| "Offer" | "Modeled terms" or "Estimated terms" |
| "Approval" (implied by LendPaper) | "Subject to lender approval" |
| "Guaranteed" | Never use in any financial context |
| "Proposal" (binding connotation) | "Preliminary estimate" |
| "LendPaper recommends..." | "This scenario shows..." |
| "You qualify for..." | Remove. Never implied. |
| "LendPaper Inc." / "LendPaper LLC." | "LendPaper" (until incorporation confirmed) |
