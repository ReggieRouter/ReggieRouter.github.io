Core Rollout Rules & Compliance Guidelines

▼ Expand Rules

📋 Underwriting Copy Standards

  • ⚠️ MCA Compliance: Merchant Cash Advances are strictly purchases of future receivables, NOT loans. Do NOT use loan terminology like "interest", "APR", or "interest rate". Cost per dollar is referred to as "factor rate" or "factor cost".
  • 🏛️ Nomenclature: Limit product types to canonical sentence-case names: Term Loan, MCA, Line of Credit, Equipment, SBA, Factoring.
  • 🛡️ Stacking Position Control: Position stacking analysis restricts max allowed positions to 4 to prevent deal dilution. Adjust stacked positions cautiously.
  • 🖼️ Brand Logo Integrity: Verify whether the 🟢 Icon Live indicator is active. If missing, local SVG fallback letters will represent the brand.
  • 🌍 Legal Anchors: California and New York rules require explicit context-aware tooltips for effective APR representation on standard loans.

🐝 Beehive Pipeline & Sync Rules

  • ⚡ Drift-Safety Rule: When Beehive crawls updates that conflict with the database, it saves the old values into a previous_data backup and resets status to pending, keeping active matching safe until manual validation.
  • 🚫 Zero-Value Protection: Crawled 0 or empty values **cannot overwrite** existing positive thresholds. The editor automatically blocks these failed scrapes to prevent data corruption.
  • 🎛️ Field-by-Field Selectivity: Underwriters can approve, reject, or customize inputs field-by-field. Selecting Currently Displayed At reverts that specific field, while Update To adopts the scraped change.
  • 🔄 Self-Healing Baseline: Once changes are approved/saved, the database baseline is reset. Beehive's subsequent scrapes compare against this new approved baseline automatically.
  • 📦 Logo Scraping Block: New brand logo assets downloads are permanently disabled inside the pipeline to respect asset licensing. Restored assets reside under local paths.
